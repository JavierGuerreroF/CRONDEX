Introduction

Neurodevelopmental disorders (NDDs) represent a significant social challenge. Understanding their genetic and epigenetic bases is crucial for advancing treatment options. CRONDEX provides an integrated resource for researchers and healthcare professionals, offering insights into the chromatin-related genes associated with NDDs.

Features

  • Gene-centered queries: Retrieve phenotype and disease associations.
  • Disease-centered queries: Identify associated genes and pathways.
  • Interactive visualizations: Heatmaps, enrichment plots, and gene networks.
  • Functional enrichment analysis: GO and KEGG pathways.
  • Custom gene list uploads and result downloads.
