Welcome to NDD Prot DB

An interactive database to explore proteins, genes, and their variants associated with neurodevelopment.

Usage Instructions

  1. Genes: Search and select specific genes.
  2. Phenotypes: View phenotypes associated with the selected genes.
  3. Diseases: Explore diseases related to genetic variants.
  4. Ontology: Look up genetic and biological ontology terms.
  5. Variants: Visualize and analyze specific genetic variants.

Objectives

Facilitate genetic research in neurodevelopment.

Provide a tool for analyzing genetic variants.

Contribute to the understanding of neurodevelopmental disorders.
